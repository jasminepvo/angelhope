Our Founder Story

The Beginning of [name]

Back in 2017, Liv embarked on their first solo trip as a sophomore in college. In the process, they fell in love with the freedom of traveling alone.

On every trip, after exploring the museums and the parks and the hiking trails, the time would inevitably arrive to decide; “where am I going out tonight?” This was often coupled with another underlying, pressing question. “How do I know the place I choose will be safe?” After backpacking through destinations in the US, Central America, Europe, and Asia, it became clear that often the only solution was to check Google Reviews, and then roll the dice. Sometimes that worked. Sometimes it didn’t.

Research Backed

It appeared that she wasn’t alone. According to a 2018 study run by the Center on Gender Equity and Health, nightlife is the fourth most common place for sexual harassment across all identities, surpassed only by ‘ALL public spaces’, ‘work’, and ‘home’. And there was little push to change the culture that fostered this environment, outside of oases like Brooklyn and DC. During this time, Liv was also working deeply in the field of social entrepreneurship. They successfully founded conferences and ran case competitions around the topic, earning fellowships from organizations like Rockefeller Philanthropy Advisors, and even served as the Advisory Board Chair for a $5.9M venture fund.

The Goal

In their eventual managerial role at MassChallenge, a world-renowned nonprofit startup accelerator, they learned directly from industry leaders on how to successfully build and scale a startup, all while staying true to the mission. After leaving MassChallenge, Liv has dedicated all of their time to solving this epidemic of sexual harassment in nightlife. They know that by leveraging both community and tech, we can ensure a fun night and a safe night go hand in hand.
