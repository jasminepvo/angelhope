How AngelHope Works

AngelHope is aimed to protect YOU and provide all women/non-binary with a safe environment surrounded by personnel who have been trained to measures when danger arises. Our hope is ALSO to make sure you have an amazing, fun experience with in-app promos curteous to our partnered venues!

Location

Use our in-app map to find partnered venues near you. Each of these venues have trained their staff to handle sexual harassment and assault, and are committed to keeping your night fun and safe.

Reporting

With a tap of a button, let on-site staff know if you ever feel unsafe or uncomfortable. Using your user profile and precise geolocation, staff will find you and deescalate the situation. Your safety is the priority.

Promos

While safety is a priority, fun is a must! Discover events happening tonight at partnered venues nearby, and take advantage of in-app promos like free drinks or free entry.
